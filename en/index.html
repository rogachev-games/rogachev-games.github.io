About me

A former entrepreneur from Saint Petersburg who decided to turn his life around.

Now I am a freelancer, game designer, and indie developer. I have expertise in developing games for PC and mobile platforms. I love technical and systems design, world-building, and balance order. I have consulted students on courses such as "Advanced Game Design," "Narrative Games," "Game Project Pre-production," "Balance and Economy," and "Unreal Engine Blueprints."

I have a higher education degree in "Business Informatics," an official qualification as a game designer, and an award in 3D graphics.

I am always looking for new and interesting projects. If you think this describes you, send me an email! ✉️

If you have any other questions, feel free to reach out!
